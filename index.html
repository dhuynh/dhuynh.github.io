STiC

My first design project in my engineering undergrad career. We were given opportunity to target any space within the mechanical realm and solve a problem. We wanted to allow people with disabilities to be able to ski normally. The STiC device clamps on to each ski respectively and holds the ski in a V position. Individuals with weaker leg muscles will be able to control their direction more easily and thus, be able to ski! The idea and design is extremely close to a guitar capo.

  • Date: November 2012
